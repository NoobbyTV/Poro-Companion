RIFTBOUND
Companion App

A personal companion app for the Riftbound TCG by Riot Games. Browse cards, track your collection, build decks, log games, and track points during matches.

Card Gallery

Browse all Riftbound cards with search, domain/type/rarity/set filters, and sort options. Tap any card for full details including energy, power, text, and artist.

Collection Tracker

Track which cards you own and how many copies. Stats bar shows unique count, total cards, and completion percentage. Add cards with a fullscreen overlay.

Deck Builder

Stepped flow: Legend → Champion → 40 Main Deck → 3 Battlefields → 12 Runes → 8 Sideboard. Enforces domain identity and 3-copy limit across all sections.

Card Scanner

Scan physical cards to identify them and see estimated prices. Add scanned cards to your collection. Track scan session history and total value.

Game Tracker

Log game results with your deck, opponent's legend, win/loss, dice roll outcome, and notes. Per-deck W/L breakdown and overall win rate stats.

Point Tracker

Fullscreen split-screen life counter for 2/3/4 players. Opponent panel rotated 180° for across-the-table play. Built-in D20 dice roller. Supports 8, 9, and 11 point targets.

Deck Building Rules Enforced

Domain Identity

Legend defines 2 domains. All main deck, sideboard, and rune cards must match these domains.

Deck Structure

1 Legend + 1 Champion + 40 Main Deck + 3 Battlefields + 12 Runes + 0 or 8 Sideboard.

Copy Limit

Maximum 3 copies of any card across main deck, sideboard, and chosen champion combined.

Champion Matching

Chosen Champion must match the Legend's champion identity and domain colors.
